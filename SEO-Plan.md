# SEO Action Plan — signal-to-noise.co

*Drafted 2026-06-01. Translates a generic SEO audit into a site-specific, codebase-anchored plan. Phases are independent — pick à la carte; nothing here requires sequential execution.*

---

## What's already shipped (don't redo)

Most of the technical foundation a typical SEO audit recommends is already in place on this site:

- **Open Graph + Twitter card** meta tags on every page (`src/layouts/BaseLayout.astro:42-53`)
- **Canonical URL** on every page (`BaseLayout.astro:40`)
- **XML sitemap** auto-generated by `@astrojs/sitemap` at `/sitemap-index.xml`
- **RSS feed** at `/rss.xml`
- **Per-article frontmatter** with `title`, `excerpt`, `date`, `tags`
- **Mobile-responsive** layout via Tailwind
- **Cover images compressed to WebP** at q=82 (e.g. the IPO piece: 2.1MB source → 122KB delivered)
- **Clean URL slugs** (`/insights/<slug>/`)
- **Internal cross-references between articles** — after the 2026-06-01 cleanup, every cross-reference to another article in the corpus is a local `/insights/...` link, not a LinkedIn URL

## The real gaps

Three categories, ordered by impact:

1. **Metadata is generic.** Homepage `<title>` reads `signal-to-noise.co — Himanshu` rather than something a search engine can match to "CFO AI strategy" / "finance storytelling" / "capital structure". Cheapest fix; largest immediate lift.
2. **No topic structure.** Articles carry tags in frontmatter, but there are no tag-index pages, no pillar pages, and no "Topics" navigation. Search engines and humans can't land on "everything I've written about AI" or "everything about capital structure".
3. **No measurement loop.** No analytics; no Google Search Console verification. Everything past Phase 1 is guesswork without this.

## Honest read

The generic SEO advice on "8–12 target keywords" and "keyword-aware intros" is real but easy to overdo. The site's actual moat is **voice + judgment**, not keyword density. The two of those that genuinely matter here:

- **Clear audience positioning** (CFOs / finance leaders / operators) — so the right humans find it
- **Authority signals** (named author, real bio, real corpus depth) — so search engines trust the site on those topics

Everything else is hygiene. Important hygiene, but hygiene.
