When companies grow, the instinct is to do more: More projects. More hires. More initiatives.

But the companies that scale successfully are the ones disciplined enough to stop:

-   Stop pet projects
-   Stop outdated processes
-   Stop legacy sacred cows
-   Stop meetings with no purpose
-   Stop chasing every opportunity
-   Stop confusing activity with progress

Growth creates clutter. Leadership creates clarity.

The most successful companies don’t win by adding more. They win by choosing what not to do.
