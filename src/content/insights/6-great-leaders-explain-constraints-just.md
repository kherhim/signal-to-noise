Every leader has to decline ideas, delay projects, or tighten resources.

But here’s what separates high-trust leaders from the rest:

**They explain the WHY.**

Not in financial jargon. Not with defensiveness. But with context, clarity, and respect.

When people understand the trade-offs behind constraints, they stay aligned and motivated. When they don’t, they assume politics or indifference.

I’ve seen morale collapse not because of hard decisions — but because of unexplained decisions.

Constraint is not the problem. Silence is.
