Resilience is often misunderstood as stubbornness or endurance.

But real resilience — the type that keeps companies alive — comes from optionality:

- **Cash buffers**
- **Talent depth**
- **Flexible cost base**
- **Technology that scales**
- **Diversified revenue**
- **Strong governance**

Toughness is a mindset. Optionality is a strategy.

As CFO, I’ve learned that resilience is built long before you need it.

You cannot be resilient at the moment you require resilience.
