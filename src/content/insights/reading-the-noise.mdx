Most finance writing is one of two things: a quarterly earnings rehash, or a hot take with no model behind it. The first is data without thinking. The second is thinking without data. The space between them — *signal* — is where I want to write.

This site collects three things:

- **Insights** — strategy, problem-framing, and pattern-spotting across finance, technology, digital assets, and trading.
- **Case studies** — substantive work, anonymised where it has to be.
- **About** — who I am, what I've built, what I'm looking for next.

If you're here because you're evaluating me — for an advisory seat or a partnership — start with the [case studies](/case-studies). If you're here for the writing, [the insights index](/insights) is the front door.

I won't post on a cadence. I'll post when I have something worth saying. That's the deal.
